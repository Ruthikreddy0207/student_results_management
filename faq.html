Frequently Asked Questions

Students

How can I view my results?

Log in to your student account and navigate to the 'Results' section to view your results.

How can I download my results?

After viewing your results, you will find an option to download them as a PDF.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Admin

How can I add new users?

Log in to the admin account and navigate to the 'Manage Users' section to add new users.

How can I edit user credentials?

In the 'Manage Users' section, you can select a user and edit their credentials as required.

Can I delete a user account?

Yes, you can delete a user account from the 'Manage Users' section in the admin dashboard.

Staff

How do I upload student results?

Log in to your staff account and navigate to the 'Upload Results' section to upload student results via an Excel sheet.

What format should the Excel sheet be in?

The Excel sheet should contain columns for student ID, name, subject, grade, and semester.

How can I view uploaded results?

You can view the uploaded results in the 'View Results' section of your staff account.
